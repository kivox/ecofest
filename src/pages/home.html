Welcome to Eco Fest

A family-friendly, one-day music festival that combines the best acts and musicians with a commitment to preserving the environment.

Date: Third Saturday in July

Featured Acts

Everyone's Environment

A local four-piece indie band with a passion for the environment.

Solar Drum

An energetic pop singer focused on solar-powered performances.

Green Gizmos

A children's six-piece act that uses puppets and songs to engage and educate about the environment.

DJ John Alfred

A well-known DJ dedicated to playing eco-conscious music and supporting green initiatives.

Our Green Initiatives

  • Waste Separation: Bins for paper, plastic, glass, and general waste.
  • Solar Power: Solar panels provide power for all aspects of the festival.
  • Carbon Offsetting: Tree-planting to offset carbon emissions.
